Barcelona High School, Spain

This project was an incredibly immersive experience done in full collaboration with the students themselves. We ran workshops where the kids shared their own visual interpretations of the school’s 10 core values, like community and innovation. Based on all their ideas and imagination, we created custom mockups and brought their stories to life on the school walls.

Whether we’re painting a café, office, school or private home, our goal is always the same: create artwork that belongs there—not just visually, but emotionally.

Dionysus Vineyard, Croatia

Wine carries stories through generations. We wanted the walls to do the same.

Inspired by the mythology of Dionysus and the surrounding vineyards, this mural connects the winery’s history with its landscape through expressive illustration and rich organic forms.

Created in close collaboration with the owners, the artwork welcomes visitors long before the first glass is poured.
