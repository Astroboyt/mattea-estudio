We’re Matt &Tea

Artists. Designers. Storytellers.

Welcome to Mattea Production! We are Mat and Thea, partners in both life and art. Together, our styles merge to perfectly balance technical street art with creative playfulness. Our true specialty is custom, tailor-made art. We don’t just paint random designs; we focus on creating concepts drawn directly from your stories. Whether it is a mural for a school, a business, or a custom canvas painting, our goal is to take your vision and bring it to life in the real world. We are here to help you elevate your space and make it completely unforgettable.

Our journey started when we met and realized that blending our completely different backgrounds created something really special. Matt is a street artist from South Africa with 15 years of experience working on diverse mural projects, bringing the technical skills and raw energy. Tea is an art teacher and the founder of a creative studio in Barcelona, bringing the playfulness, structure, and imagination.

Meet the studio

Dionysus Vineyard, Croatia

We were hired by a private vineyard owner in Croatia who wanted a standout piece to attract visitors to their wine cellar. Mat painted the head of Dionysus, the god of wine and celebration, not just as a striking focal point, but as a nod to bringing a good harvest.

The mural was painted on-the-spot while we were traveling and staying close by during our holidays.

What You Need to Know

Share Your Vision

Begin by sharing your vision with us. Do you have any reference images or ideas in mind? We’d love to hear about them.

Provide Essential Details

To help us better understand your project, please provide the following information:

  • Photographs of the space
  • Dimensions or size of the project
  • Time-frame for completion
  • Number of walls involved

Discuss Your Budget

If you have a budget in mind, please let us know upfront. This helps us tailor our creative solutions to meet your financial expectations.

Visualize Your Concept

Before any paint touches the surface, we can create sketches and digital mock-ups based on our discussions. This step ensures that we’re aligned with your vision.

Secure Your Project

To get started, we require a deposit. This commitment allows us to schedule your project and begin the creative process.

Our goal is to make your vision a reality, and this process helps us achieve that. We’re here to answer any questions and guide you through each step. Let’s bring your project to life together!

Questions, Answers

Every mural is different. Price depends on size, surface, level of detail and location—after a first conversation we’ll send a clear quote with no surprises.

Most walls take between a few days and two weeks on site, plus concept time beforehand. We’ll give you a realistic timeline with the quote.

We start with a conversation, then move into sketches and concepts based on your story. Once we’re aligned, we bring it to life on the wall—from the first line to the final detail.
